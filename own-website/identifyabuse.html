What is abuse?

Abuse is when someone causes us harm or distress. It can take many forms, ranging from disrespect to causing someone physical or mental pain. It can occur in someone's home, a care home, a hospital or a public place. Often the people who commit abuse are taking advantage of a special relationship.It can be physical, emotional, financial, sexual, or digital, and it often involves patterns of power and control over a victim.


Signs of Abuse

  • Physical Abuse: Unexplained bruises, injuries, fearfulness.
  • Emotional Abuse: Manipulation, gaslighting, isolation.
  • Financial Abuse: Controlling money, preventing independence.
  • Digital Abuse: Stalking, harassment, monitoring online activity.

    • What to do if you suspect abuse

  • Document incidents.
